## Three motions the AI layer makes

1. **Predictive alerts** — surface what's about to break, not after. Cohort churn, inventory drift, payment latency.
2. **Natural-language queries** — ask in plain Japanese or English. Bilingual, JIS-aware, cited responses.
3. **Next-best-action suggestions** — agent drafts, your merchandiser approves. One click to ship.

::right::

### Why it works

Not a separate dashboard. Not a chatbot. **Pre-integrated** into the merchandising and customer-care tools your team uses every day.

The three motions only work because the AI layer sits on **one data model across all modules**. Other platforms bolt AI onto a federation of stores; QORTEX's AI reads from the same source the modules write to.
